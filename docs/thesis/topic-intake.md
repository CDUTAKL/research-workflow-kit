# Topic Intake

## Update Rules

- Use this file when the advisor gives a title or fixed topic.
- Preserve the advisor's original wording before rewriting or narrowing it.
- Mark uncertain interpretations as `TBD` and confirm them with the advisor.
- After topic intake, update `thesis-brief.md`, `project-scope-control.md`, `writing-outline.md`, `literature-matrix.md`, and `experiment-architecture.md`.
- Treat a provided title as a research hypothesis until `project-scope-control.md` confirms title survival or narrowing.
